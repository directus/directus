# AI Usage Policy for Contributors

## Overview

We welcome the use of AI tools (e.g. LLM) to assist with development in this repository. However, AI is a tool, not a
substitute for understanding or accountability.

Contributors are responsible for any code they submit. Maintainers are responsible for any code that is reviewed,
merged, and released. To protect the integrity of this project, we maintain a high bar for all contributions.

## Use of AI in Code Contributions

AI may be used to assist with writing, reviewing, or improving code. However:

- You must fully understand any AI-assisted code you submit
- You are responsible for verifying correctness, security, and performance
- Submissions must meet all project standards and guidelines

We require a **human in the loop** at all times.

The use of **fully autonomous agents** to create issues, pull requests, or other contributions is **not allowed**.
Contributions suspected to be generated without meaningful human involvement will be closed.

## Communication Guidelines

All communication in this repository must be written by humans.

- Do **not** use AI to generate issue descriptions, pull request text, comments, or replies
- Do **not** paste AI-generated responses when interacting with maintainers
- Comments that appear to be AI-generated may be removed

We expect contributors to communicate clearly in their own words.

### Issues

When opening an issue:

- Describe the problem in your own words
- Include relevant details, context, and reproduction steps
- Avoid copying AI-generated explanations

### Pull Requests

When submitting a pull request:

- Clearly explain the purpose and scope of your changes in your own words
- Be prepared to explain your implementation and reasoning
- Respond to feedback thoughtfully and independently

## Referencing AI Output

If you include AI-generated content for context:

- Clearly disclose that it is AI-generated
- Use a quote block (e.g., `>`)
- Provide your own explanation of why it is relevant
- Keep excerpts short and focused

## Non-Native English Speakers

We recognize that contributors may use AI tools to improve clarity or translate text.

- You may use AI for grammar or language assistance
- Ensure the final message reflects your own intent and understanding
- For translations, consider including:
  - Your original text
  - The AI-generated translation in a quote block

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take the following actions if this policy is not followed:

- Request clarification or revisions
- Remove or hide comments
- Close issues or pull requests
